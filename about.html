About Moremi

Moremi Techstars Foundation is a non-profit organization and we are committed to making Nigeria the country with the highest percentage of women in the technology workforce. we encourage all generations of girls and women to write their future like the great Moremi Ajasoro. Moremi Techstars Foundation is inspired by an energetic and forward-thinking team that primarily consists of volunteers who are working full-time jobs, raising families, and have a passion for improving the lives of girls and women in Nigeria through technology. According to  UNESCO Science Report 2021 found women are still under-represented in the fields of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics).

The vast majority of tech developers are males, accounting for 91.7 percent of all respondents. Female developers amounted to only five percent of all respondents, demonstrating the male-dominated reality of software jobs.

When one woman helps another, amazing things can happen and progress for women is progress for all. We believe in a world where access to knowledge, innovation and business opportunities are based on interest and ability, not gender.

The aim is to educate, equip and empower women and girls with the necessary skills and confidence to succeed in the technological world. We promote girls’ and women’s empowerment in Africa, with a focus on Sustainable Development. Everything we do at Moremi Techstars centers on inspiring and growing women in technology. We work to broadly reach young girls to get them to consider technology and deeply connect with women who choose the technology path.

We tend to increase the number of girls and women in the fields of technology / engineering. Our Vision

To create an avenue where women are equally represented and thrive across all career roles in Technology.

Our mission

• To build a community that embodies technical growth, networking, mentorship and visibility amongst all level career roles in Technology.
• Increase the number of girls and women in the fields of technology and engineering
• Inspiring the women of tomorrow, today in technology.
• to help women consider, advance and stay in tech
• Giving a woman a voice for voice to achieve sustainability and inclusion in the tech space.
